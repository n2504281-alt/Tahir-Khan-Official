THE VISIONARY

Founder Story & Journey

Leadership Philosophy

Tahir Khan's career as an entrepreneur is rooted in authentic relationships and uncompromising quality. Starting with a singular restaurant, his strategic vision focused on building a premium environment where hospitality represents an act of respect rather than a commercial transaction.

"Serve people the kind of food you would proudly serve your own guests at home."

This philosophy—treating guests as 'mehmaan' (family)—defines the operational standards across his entire restaurant group and guided TKR to win the prestigious 'Best Restaurant of the Year 2023' award.

Tahir Khan's dedication to regional goodwill went viral globally when he hosted an Indian tennis family visiting Islamabad for a tournament, welcoming them as his personal guests. By offering them traditional cuisine, Tahir demonstrated that hospitality knows no boundaries.
